Welcome to RUBRIC!

Rutland's Unified Budget Review and Information Center provides a high-level overview of the town’s budget and financial position. This portal is intended to be a tool for the community to quickly access prior years spending and proposed budget information. Below you’ll find information related to the proposed revenue and expense budgets. This portal also contains extensive detail on historical General Fund revenues, expenses, reserve positions, and forecasted future expenses. Users can also benchmark Rutland in comparison to similar communities in Massachusetts. The Town's Capital Improvement Plan and Enterprise Funds will be added over time. We hope you find this portal a useful tool to access the Town's financial information in the interest of transparency.
