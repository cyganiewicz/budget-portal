Reserves Funds Overview

Free Cash Reserve

The Free Cash Reserve is maintained to ensure sufficient liquidity for day-to-day operations and unforeseen expenses.

General Stabilization Reserve

The General Stabilization Reserve is used to stabilize the municipality’s finances during economic downturns or revenue shortfalls.

Capital Stabilization Reserve

The Capital Stabilization Reserve is set aside for long‑term capital projects and infrastructure investments.
